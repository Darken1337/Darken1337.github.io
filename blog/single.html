The Criptex cryptocurrency platform developed own bonus system.

We divided our platform into 5 levels to generate maximum benefits to traders. Lower fee, more earned bonuses and more earnings awaits you at every level.

How can a user get a new level? You get bonuses almost for every action on the exchange. It's enough for you to earn the required number of bonuses, if you would like moving to a next level.

You have a chance to get a level with the minimum fee of 0,01%.
You can also buy levels 3, 4 and 5. The main principle of our 5-level bonus system: the higher your level is, the less you fee is. The users of any level constantly get bonuses for actions on the exchange:

  • bonuses for every transaction on the exchange;
  • bonuses for deposit of funds;
  • bonuses for the first level referral bidding;
  • bonuses for the second level referral bidding

The more funds involved in a transaction or deposited to the exchange, the more operation bonuses you get. So, active traders can achieve to the desired level and reduce fees on the Criptex exchange.
